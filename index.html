About Me

Here is the story of how Bluetiful came to be.

My father always told me I could be anything I wanted to be. My father believed in me and he had big dreams for me. I however had other dreams.

When I was about 3 years old, my father was out in the yard building a picket fence and I said, "When I grow up I'm going to be a clown. I can even perform magic. Look, I can walk on nails."

The next thing I remember was waking up with bandages on my foot. That was the start of the Bluetiful Life...

I didn't think about being a clown until my son Miguel was in kindergarten and about to turn 6 years old. I wanted to hire a clown for his party but I soon found out that a clown was not affordable to me.

I thought to myself, "I could be the clown." with the help of my sweet friend, Janet Fry, we were the hit at the party. We had a lot of FUN! Thank you Janet.

It was after this I met Frannie the clown outside Gypsy Treasure, who right away became my BFF (my Best Frannie Friend) . Frannie agreed to teach me how to face paint and twist balloons. We soon began painting the world together. I learned a lot from her and became passionate about being the best clown I could be. I was determined and practiced day and night. I still take online classes and attend jams to get better and better.

I find so much enjoyment in every face paint, balloon and glitter tattoo that I do. Please check out my gallery links. Thanks for stopping by!

To my dad (in memory) and to my three children. I hope I make you proud!
